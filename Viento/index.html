About Us

Viento is an initiative to switch to non-conventional sources of energy by using the energy getting wasted from automobiles in an effective way. Considering the present scenario where depletion of fossil fuels have become a major issue and we are still on the way to get used to the renewable sources of energy, we have come up with a productive approach to generate energy from the wind caused due to motion of a vehicle. One of the many drawbacks of any vehicle, be it motorized such as cars or non-motorized ones such as bicycles, is that we always end up “spending” energy without getting absolutely nothing in return in terms of energy. So, we have designed an Advanced Automotive Sustainable Wind Energy-based Harnessing System, which can in fact reduce the net total energy spent by actually generating energy from the vehicle’s very own kinetic energy. As an incentive towards a green and clean earth, we are determined to build a sustainable future.

F.A.Q

  • How will this project impact the general public?

    For people living in rural areas where electricity is still a major issue, we plan on distributing these models to those who commute daily on their bicycles to work, thus offering a helping hand to the people in need.

  • Who are the taget consumers of the product?

    One of the biggest advantages of our project is that it can be used with any kind of vehicle (excluding the tube model) be it a cycle, a motor bike or a car. Even heavy vehicles such as trucks can use this product to their own advantage. Therefore, has a very wide customer range.

  • How is the project different from other wind power vehicles?

    Firstly,it utilizes the vehicles own velocity to generate energy and is the first of its kind to be launched in the market. Secondly,it can charge bigger batteries as well as power banks.
